A girl named Nox follows a silent raven into an abandoned alchemist's house. She expects to find the formula that cursed her. Instead she finds the story of someone who spent his entire life asking the same question she carries.

"The formula was never meant to cure her.
It was meant to help her see herself."
Why this exists

This started as a feeling, not an idea.

I'm Amira. I'm 27, from Egypt. I spent four years building real-time physics simulations in Unity — converting biology, chemistry, and physics experiments into virtual labs for students. It was good work. But it wasn't mine.

In 2025 I decided to go back to what I actually loved: making games. And instead of making something safe, I made something honest.

Nox is a girl who feels exactly the way I sometimes feel. I didn't plan that. It just came out that way.

"The Hollow Formula is about learning that some questions do not need answers.
Some wounds do not need solutions.
And some goodbyes are not endings."

This page documents the journey — the decisions made, the things that worked, the things that didn't. Honestly.

Characters

Nox — you

Age 10–12. Dark short hair. Honey amber eyes. Dark striped blouse, short skirt, black tights, worn boots. The player never sees her — just as the world never truly saw her. She exists only through her hands reaching out, and the raven who looks back.

The Raven — silent, nameless

He belonged to the alchemist. He was there when everything happened. He never speaks. He communicates through where he sits, what he drops, and when he looks away. His story is not about guiding Nox. It is about learning to let go.

The Alchemist — never seen

Gone before the story begins. Present in every room. He felt the same curse. He spent his whole life trying to formula his way out of it. The house is his mind. Every room, a chapter of his search.
